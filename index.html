About Aga Khan In Kenya

Aga Khan Health Services(AKHS) in kenya has thrived to provide world class medical services to the people of kenya.The Aga khan hospital has then spread it exapansion to various parts of the country i.e Nairobi, Mombasa and Kisumu.The Kenyan government has invested heavily on Aga khan Facilities as it continues to make efforts towards achieving Universal Healthcare goals. Apart from the private Aga Khan sector located in Nairobi,which has proven expensive without the assistance of a comprehensive health insurance policy, the government has set aside more funds to open up other Aga Khan facilities in other counties like Nyeri ,Kericho and Meru. However, it wants to make sure that services can be afforded by everyone. With this in mind the government with the assistance of the Director for Health, AKDN & Global General Manager, AKHS, Dr Gijs Walraven has the following objectives. It aims at providing:

  • Better medical services.
  • Cheaper medical services.
  • Reducing the death rate of infants born in the country by providing much needed maternal care.

Aga Khan provides the best and up to date infrastructure, technology and equipement to ensure quality services are given to patients.

Aga Khan facilities have the world's best personel.

About Aga Khan Health Services(AKHS)

Aga khan Health Services(AKHS) is one of three agencies of the Aga Khan Development network (AKDN) working to tackle today's greatest health challenges in low-to-middle income countries. Through our focused presence in central Asia, East Africa and South Asia, AKHS operates 14 hospitals and medical centres as well as 375+ health centres ,offering one of the most comprehensive non-profit healthcare systems in the developing world.
